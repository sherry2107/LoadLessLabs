General Questions

How do i login for the first time?

All the valid users will recieve an e-mail including computer generated password, enter that password in the password area and you will be navigated to the registration page. After registering, you will be asked to enter the password of your choice. After this process you will be navigated to to the deshboard according to your role.

Why am i taken to registration page after login?

To recognize the valid users, one email including the random system generated password is sent to the user. When you will login using that password, you would be asked to register youeself as the system does not have the nacessory information about the users. After filling up all the information and password, you would be registered.

Why am I taken to reset password page after registration?

The system first sent you the random system generated password. After registering yourself, you are asked to enter the password of your choice so that you could use the password of your choice one after you are registered and using the system frequently.

Student

How do I start?

When students first logs in and registers, they will be directed to their dashboards. You will see the panel in the left section of the dashboard. Click on the option you want and that would open on the screen.

Where do I find my practicals and assignments?

In the student dashboard, there is a panel including different options which includes practicals and assignments. Choosing one of them would open the list of either practicals/assignments(i.e if you choose practicals, the list of practicals would open and if it has any sub-practicals, it would also include that.)

Where do I edit the practicals and assignments?

Click on any practical/assignment from the list and you would be provided editors as required.(i.e for assignments, you would be given the text-area.)

How do I edit my profile?

In the left panel, there is an option to edit profiles. Click on there and you would be shown your current information. Change the information you want to change and click on save.

My practicals/assignments are blocked...

Practicals and assignments are uploaded by the faculties with the due date. If the student misses the due-date, it would be blocked. the student won't be able to submit the practical/assignment one the due date has passed.

Admin

How do I start?

When Admin first logs in, they will be directed to their dashboards. You will see the panel in the left section of the dashboard. Click on the option you want and that would open on the screen.

How do I upload circulars?

Make a .doc file including the circular on your pc. Click on the option 'Upload Circulars' in side panel on your dashboard. Add the title of the circular. You will be asked to choose a file from your pc. add the .doc file. You can choose a file by clicking on 'Browse' option. After choosing, click on 'Upload File' and the file will be uploaded.

How do I Add users?

Make a file including Registration id and email of the users. Click on the option 'Add Students'/'Add Faculties' from the side panel. You will be asked to choose a file from your pc. Add the .doc file. You can choose a file by clicking on 'Browse' option. After choosing, click on 'Upload File' and users mentioned in the file will be added.

How do I remove users?

Make a file including Registration id and email of the users. Click on the option 'Remove Students'/'Remove Faculties' from the side panel. You will be asked to choose a file from your pc. Add the .doc file. You can choose a file by clicking on 'Browse' option. After choosing, click on 'Upload File' and users mentioned in the file will be removed.
